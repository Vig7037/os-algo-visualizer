Why Scheduling Algorithms Matter

The main objectives of CPU Scheduling Algorithms include:

CPU Utilization

Efficiently use CPU resources by allowing processes to share CPU time when others are waiting on I/O, maximizing system throughput.

Maximum Performance

Achieve higher CPU utilization — ideally up to 90% in heavy-load conditions — to ensure the processor is rarely idle.

Minimized Time

Reduce waiting, turnaround, and response time through well-optimized scheduling strategies tailored to different process behaviors.
